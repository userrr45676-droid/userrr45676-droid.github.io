The problem is real

More than two thirds of people have lost digital files they can never get back. Baby photos vanished in a hard drive crash. Final voicemails from loved ones, gone when an account got locked. Journals deleted when a cloud service shut down.

Google Drive and iCloud aren't built for permanence. They can lock you out, raise prices, or disappear entirely. Even blockchain solutions like Arweave are too technical for regular people and don't solve the inheritance problem.

People need something that actually works for protecting what matters.
