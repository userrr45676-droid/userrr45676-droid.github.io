What is ShadowLiquidity?

ShadowLiquidity is a non-executing MEV surveillance agent that monitors pending transactions in the mempool to detect imminent liquidations on DeFi protocols like Aave, Compound, and Morpho.

Instead of competing in expensive gas auctions, it predicts and verifies liquidation conditions off-chain using advanced simulation techniques, giving you an information advantage without execution risk.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the predictions?
Our current testnet shows 96% accuracy for liquidations within 2 blocks. We're continuously improving the model with historical data and will publish detailed accuracy metrics upon mainnet launch.
Do you execute liquidations yourself?
No. ShadowLiquidity is purely an intelligence layer. We don't execute any transactions, don't require wallet access, and don't compete in MEV auctions. You receive alerts and decide what to do with them.
What protocols do you support?
Currently: Aave V2/V3, Compound V2/V3, and Morpho Blue on Ethereum mainnet. We're adding support for Arbitrum, Optimism, and Base in Q3 2025.
How do you make money without executing?
We charge subscription fees for API access. The value we provide is the intelligence infrastructure—you don't need to build and maintain mempool monitoring, state simulation, or oracle tracking yourself.
Can I trust the alerts?
Every alert includes a cryptographic proof (Merkle path or EVM trace hash) that you can verify independently. We also track our historical accuracy on-chain, so our reputation is transparent and verifiable.
Will this be open-source?
Yes. Core detection logic will be open-sourced in Q4 2025 after we validate the approach. The API service layer will remain proprietary, but the algorithms will be public for educational purposes.
